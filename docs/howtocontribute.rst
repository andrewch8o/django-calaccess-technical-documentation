How to contribute
=================

This is an open-source project, and we actively encourage contributions from developers,
journalists, researchers, civic data enthusiasts...anyone who has the
time and energy to help us untangle the CAL-ACCESS database.

Also, you will need a `GitHub <https://github.com/>`_ account.
